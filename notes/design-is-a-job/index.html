Design is a Job

A series of quotes and paraphrases from Design is a Job by Mike Monteiro.

  • A designer solves problems within a set of constraints.
  • A designer understands goals, gathers information & imposes order.
  • The work starts before a single pixel is placed.
  • Clients are looking for confidence.
  • Lack of clients is the number one reason studios fail.
  • Be pleasant to work with, do good work.
  • All successful jobs lead to more jobs.
  • Be pleasant, don’t be nice.
  • How a client behaves at the start is how they will behave later on.
  • Trust your gut.
  • Walk away from clients who don’t listen and just want an implementer.
  • Don’t work with people who show up begrudgingly and dont value design.
  • Work for money!
  • Charge as much as you can.
  • You can always negotiate down a high price. You can’t go up from a low price.
  • Break down your bill into the services your charging for.
  • Never compete with the client to solve the problem.
  • Present your work or you are not a designer. Keep at it and you’ll get better.
  • End a presentation on time. Anwser everyones questions.
  • Never speak ill of your clients.
  • Lawyers talk to lawyers.
  • Always have a contract.
  • The larger the project the more often you should invoice.
