Jiro Dreams of Sushi

A series of ideas gleaned from the documentary film Jiro Dreams of Sushi.

  • Immerse yourself in your work.
  • Fall in love with your work.
  • Never complain about your work.
  • Dedicate your life to mastering your skill.
  • Jiro only makes sushi, no appetizers. Do one thing and do it well.
  • Make an effort and repeat the same thing every day.
  • Jiro is always looking ahead, never satisfied with his work, always trying to improve.
  • Be extremely self critical.
  • Take work seriously & constantly perform on the highest level.
  • Aspire to improve skills.
  • Work with specialists.
  • Build a relationship of trust with colleagues.
  • Jiro doesn’t care about money, only about making good sushi.
  • Always look ahead and above yourself to improve.
